# CyberChef

Welcome to the **CyberChef Kitchen**, young crypto apprentice!

## Scenario

The Michelin-starred Chef of the CyberChef Kitchen has just plated his
signature dish, the *"flag en papillote"*, and served it cold, wrapped
in `encrypted_flag.txt`. As any good chef would, he prepared it following
a strict recipe: a short pipeline of cryptographic and encoding
*ingredients*, applied one after the other in a very specific order.
The dish was cooked in three stages. Your job, as the apprentice sneaking into the kitchen after service, is to taste the dish, recognise the
ingredients, reconstruct the exact cooking order, and reverse the whole
recipe back to the original flag, which has the form `spritz{...}`.

The Chef is a traditionalist so his pantry only contains ingredients
taken from the introductory cookbook
[*"CTF Crypto for Beginners"*](https://charcharbinks.com/post/ctf_crypto_for_beginners/#url-encoding-percent-encoding).

A few whispers from the kitchen, to nudge you towards the right
direction. The outermost layer is only a thin garnish on top of the actual dish,
something the Chef added at the very end just to plate his work, and
once you peel it away the real cooking begins to show. Underneath, the
two stages are deliberately gentle on the palate. One
of them is a humble shift over the alphabet, with a key space so small
that it can comfortably be tasted one spice at a time. The other is a
slightly richer recipe with a textual key, but the Chef has good taste
and would never pick anything outside his own kitchen vocabulary: a
short, common English word, written as one would shout it across a
busy service with 7 letters.

## Deliverables

Recover the flag in the form `spritz{...}` and report the full recipe and charactiristics of each ingridient.

Happy cooking!
